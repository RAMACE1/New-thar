Frequently Asked Questions

You'll stay in a traditional village home with basic amenities, just like the locals. It's authentic, not luxurious. Expect simple bedding, shared facilities, and the genuine experience of village life.

All meals are prepared with fresh, local ingredients following traditional methods. We ensure cleanliness while maintaining authenticity. The food is the same that the host family eats daily.

Basic English is spoken, and we facilitate meaningful cultural exchange through gestures, smiles, and shared experiences. Language barriers often lead to the most heartwarming moments!
